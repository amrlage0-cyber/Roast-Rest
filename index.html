✦ About Us

A Corner Worth Returning To

Roast & Rest was born out of a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee deserves an equally great place to be enjoyed. Nestled in the heart of El Maadi, Cairo, we are a sanctuary for coffee lovers, wanderers, and everyone in between.

From our single-origin espresso to our handcrafted seasonal specials, every detail is considered with care. We don't just serve coffee — we craft moments you'll want to relive.
